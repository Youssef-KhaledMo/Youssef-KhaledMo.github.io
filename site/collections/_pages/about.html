Hello! I'm Youssef Khaled Mohamed, a passionate Machine Learning Engineer with a strong foundation in AI, Embedded Systems, and Software Development. I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from Helwan University, where I earned an overall rating of 'Very Good'.

During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to develop the Enhanced DriveGuard (EDG) System, an innovative safety solution designed to enhance driving safety by mitigating human error. My project was recognized for its excellence, securing 1st place in the "Made in Egypt" (MIE) competition among over 300 projects, and 3rd place in the Valeo mentorship program out of 511 projects.

I specialize in creating intelligent systems using Machine Learning and Embedded Systems, with experience in developing applications that range from safety solutions to interactive voice assistants. My technical expertise includes proficiency in Python, C++, Java, and Embedded C, as well as frameworks like TensorFlow, Scikit-Learn, and OpenCV.

I am currently advancing my skills further through the Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI) on the Machine Learning track, where I continue to explore new technologies and develop cutting-edge solutions.
